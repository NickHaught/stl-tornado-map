Where the money went

63115 and 63113 took the brunt of the damage and received 60% of total FEMA IHP ($34.2M of $56.4M). These ZIPs cover the hardest-hit neighborhoods: Greater Ville, The Ville, Kingsway East, Fountain Park, Baden, and Hamilton Heights.

Many of these blocks were already more than half vacant before May 16. On some streets, 70% of tornado victims had no homeowner's insurance.
